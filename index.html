Our Mission:

Personal Physical Therapy takes pride in providing patient-centered care to all of its patients. The personable approach that is implemented is key in ensuring an expeditious recovery, so you can get back to living life on YOUR terms.

Mariusz Stawowski arrived in the Tri-Cities about twenty years ago after receiving his Masters in Physical Therapy. The passion and drive for his profession resulted in him launching his own practice in downtown Richland, Washington. Additionally, he also became board certified in Orthopedic Physical Therapy to further broaden his expertise in the field. What differentiates Mariusz from other practices in the area is his personable and engaging approach he administers when working with his patients. He attributes this to having worked in various parts of the world, to name a few: Poland, Germany and New York City. Mariusz takes pride in developing and implementing a treatment plan that specifically caters to the needs of his patients.
